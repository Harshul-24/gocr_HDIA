ANNOTATIONS. ___Verse 436. Where heavenly trees with fainting blossoms blow.] Literally, the Kalpa trees, one of the five kinds which flourish in Indra's heaven. They are thus enumerated in the Amara Kosha :
पञ्चैते देवतखो मन्दारः पारिजातकः ।
सन्तानः कल्पवृक्षश्च पुंसि वा हरिचन्दनम् ॥ Verse 438. The city of the gods impend.] Alakā, the capital of Kuvera.
